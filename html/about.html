What is Yoga?

Derived from the Sanskrit word 'Yuj', which literally translates to "union", Yoga is the holistic approach to harmoniously integrate the body, the mind and the spirit. The earliest known mention of Yoga comes in Rig Veda, an ancient Indian collection of sacred texts including the rituals, practices, hymns and mantras. Today, yoga is practiced widely, all over the world, to release stress, to calm the mind, to be fit, to develop spiritually etc.

Yoga for everyone.

Contrary to popular belief, yoga is way more than bodies attempting complicated postures or 'asanas'. Yoga is a multidimensional practice including breathing exercises or 'pranayama', meditation techniques, visualization practices, chants and yoga asanas. Start with any one or practice them all according to your comfort, you are sure to reap the benefits of Yoga.
